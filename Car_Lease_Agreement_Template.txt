**Lease Agreement Templates**

Uses publicly available car lease agreement templates as reference documents.

Templates help understand the standard structure and commonly used legal language in lease contracts.

Collected from:

Automobile manufacturer finance websites

Banks and financial institutions

Publicly available sample lease agreements

Common sections analyzed in templates:

Lease duration and start/end dates

Monthly payment and payment schedule

Interest rate / Annual Percentage Rate (APR)

Mileage allowance and excess mileage charges

Down payment and security deposit

Maintenance and insurance responsibilities

Early termination and cancellation clauses

Penalties, late fees, and additional charges

These templates are used to:

Design the SLA extraction logic

Test OCR and AI extraction accuracy

Identify common clause patterns across contracts
