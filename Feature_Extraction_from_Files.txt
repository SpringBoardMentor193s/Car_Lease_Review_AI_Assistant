**Feature Extraction from PDF or Image**

Supports contract uploads in:

PDF format (digital or scanned)

Image format (JPG, PNG, etc.)

Uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to convert non-editable documents into machine-readable text.

Handles:

Scanned contracts

Mobile photos of documents

Multi-page PDFs

After OCR, the extracted text is processed using Large Language Models (LLMs) to identify key contractual and financial features.

Key features extracted include:

Interest rate (APR)

Lease or loan tenure

Monthly installment amount

Total payable cost over contract period

Mileage limits and overage charges

Early termination conditions and fees

Late payment penalties

Hidden or additional charges

Extracted features are:

Converted into structured data (JSON)

Stored in the database

Displayed in a user-friendly summary format
